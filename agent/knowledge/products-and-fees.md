## Fee Summary (Step 17 — Important Note)

### Fall-Below Fee
- **Triggers when:** Monthly Average Balance drops below **₹10,000**
- **Charge:** 6% of the shortfall, up to a maximum of **₹500**
- Example: If MAB is ₹8,000, the shortfall is ₹2,000. The charge is 6% of ₹2,000 = ₹120.
- Example: If MAB is ₹0, the shortfall is ₹10,000. 6% of ₹10,000 = ₹600, but the cap is ₹500, so the charge is ₹500.

### DBS Visa Classic Debit Card — Annual Fee
- **Fee:** ₹199 per year
- **Waived if any one of the following is met:**
  - Yearly debit card spending ≥ **₹1,00,000**, OR
  - Monthly Average Balance ≥ **₹50,000**, OR
  - Total Relationship Value (TRV) ≥ **₹2,00,000**
- If none of these thresholds is met, the ₹199 annual fee applies.

---

## TRV — Total Relationship Value

Verbatim definition from Step 17:

> *"TRV is across all accounts in a family and calculated by aggregating average quarterly saving account balances and end of period term deposits and investments."*

## Funding Window

- The app recommends funding the account with ₹25,000 within **30 days** of opening to avail maximum welcome benefits.
- You are not required to fund immediately on the day of account opening.
