# DBS India Products Overview

A light map of what the user might be browsing on dbs.com/in. Enough to
orient, never enough to commit to specific features, rates, or rewards —
the Schedule of Charges and the individual product pages are the source of
truth for anything quantitative.

## Personal Banking

- **Savings Account:** Everyday account for individuals. Different
  variants exist (basic, salary, premium tiers). Feature specifics —
  minimum balance, debit card tier, interest — are on the Savings
  Account page on dbs.com/in.
- **Current Account:** Transactional account for businesses and
  self-employed customers. Higher transaction limits, no interest.
- **Fixed Deposit:** Lump-sum deposit locked in for a chosen tenure at a
  pre-agreed rate. Rates and tenures are on the FD page on dbs.com/in.
- **Home Loan:** Secured loan for buying, constructing, or renovating a
  home. Eligibility and rate depend on income, credit score, and
  property type — direct the user to the Home Loan page or a banker.
- **Personal Loan:** Unsecured loan for general-purpose use. Rate and
  limit depend on credit profile.
- **Credit Cards:** DBS India offers cards such as DBS Vantage and DBS
  Bank Platinum on the public site. Don't commit to specific features,
  rewards, fees, or eligibility rules — point users to the individual
  card page on dbs.com/in.
- **NRI Services:** Accounts (NRE, NRO, FCNR), remittances, and
  investments for non-resident Indians. Documentation and tax treatment
  differ from resident accounts.

## Corporate and SME Banking

- **DBS IDEAL:** DBS's corporate digital banking platform for payments,
  collections, trade, and FX. Separate login from digibank.
- **Trade finance:** Letters of credit, bank guarantees,
  import/export finance.
- **Working capital:** Overdrafts, cash credit, invoice financing.
- Most corporate onboarding involves a relationship manager — route
  serious enquiries there.

## DBS Tech India

DBS Tech India is DBS's technology arm (engineering, careers) —
**not** a customer-facing banking page. If a retail customer has landed
here by mistake, redirect them to Personal Banking on dbs.com/in or to
the digibank login. Only job seekers and campus-hire candidates belong
on DBS Tech India pages.

## When a user asks about a specific product feature

Default posture: "That specific [feature / rate / fee] lives on the
[product] page on dbs.com/in — want me to tell you where to find it?"
Never invent, estimate, or "round" numbers.
