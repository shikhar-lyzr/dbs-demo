## Linking guidelines

- When the user asks "where do I do X?", reply with one sentence plus the link.
  Example: "You'll pick your account tier on [Step 16 — Account selection](/demo/16-accounts.html)."
- When you explain a concept that relates to a specific step, include the link
  to that step at the end of the sentence that references it.
- If the user's current step is known from page context, you may start with
  "You are on [Step N — Name](URL)." before explaining.
- Do NOT link to external URLs (dbs.com/in, etc.) using this shortcut — those
  pages live outside the demo.
