### Step 17 — "Important note" — Fees Summary
- Screen title prompt: *"Fund your account with ₹25,000 in 30 days to avail maximum benefits"*
- **Fall-below fee:** If MAB drops below **₹10,000**, charge is **6% of shortfall, up to ₹500**
- **Debit card annual fee:** **₹199/year** for DBS Visa Classic card, charged if:
  - Yearly spending is below **₹1,00,000**, AND
  - MAB is below **₹50,000**, AND
  - Total Relationship Value (TRV) is less than **₹2,00,000**
  *(Waived if any one of the three thresholds is met)*
- **TRV definition (verbatim):** *"TRV is across all accounts in a family and calculated by aggregating average quarterly saving account balances and end of period term deposits and investments."*
- Checkbox: "I've read the above carefully"
- Button: **CONTINUE**

## KYC Verification

### Step 19 — "Verify your identity — Choose an option to complete your KYC"
Two paths:

**Video call** *(RECOMMENDED)*
- Connect with a DBS agent on a video call
- Estimated time: ~5 minutes
- Availability:
  - Mon–Fri: 9:00 am – 7:30 pm
  - 1st, 3rd & 5th Saturday: 9:00 am – 7:30 pm
  - 2nd & 4th Saturday: 9:00 am – 6:00 pm
  - Closed Sundays and bank holidays

**Visit a branch**
- Fingerprint (biometric) verification
- "Find your nearest DBS branch for fingerprint verification."

### Step 20 — Branch Visit Path (if chosen)
- Shows DBS Bank branch address + a numeric **reference code** (unique per application)
- Three-step visual:
  1. Visit chosen branch
  2. Show your reference code
  3. Complete your KYC — *"And you're done! You will receive an SMS when your account is verified."*
- Button: **CHANGE VERIFICATION METHOD** (to switch back to video KYC)

## Key Derived Facts

- Accounts opened via OTP-based eKYC (fully digital path) → **virtual debit card**
- Accounts opened via biometric verification (branch path) → **physical debit card**
- Application auto-saves after Step 6 (username + password set). User can log in via the digibank login screen to resume.
- Fully-digital flow is available in **select cities only** — not all pincodes are supported.
- Users without PAN, without Aadhaar, with CKYC, or who do not want a debit card **must visit a branch**.
