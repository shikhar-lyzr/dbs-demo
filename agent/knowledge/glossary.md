# Glossary — DBS digiSavings Onboarding

Plain-language definitions for terms a user encounters during DBS digibank India's savings account onboarding flow. No specific numbers — defer to DBS pages for anything quantitative not already documented in this knowledge base.

---

## Identity and KYC

- **PAN (Permanent Account Number):** A 10-character alphanumeric tax ID issued by India's Income Tax Department. Required for the digital onboarding flow. Your name and date of birth on PAN are used to pre-fill registration fields.

- **Aadhaar:** A 12-digit unique ID issued by UIDAI. Used for eKYC (identity and address verification) during account opening. Your full Aadhaar number is sensitive — only enter it in official, secure app fields.

- **VID (Virtual ID):** A temporary 16-digit number generated from your Aadhaar by UIDAI. Can be submitted in place of your actual Aadhaar number for authentication. Generated via UIDAI's resident portal or the link shown in Step 8 of the app.

- **UIDAI (Unique Identification Authority of India):** The government body that issues and manages Aadhaar numbers and operates the eKYC authentication infrastructure.

- **KYC (Know Your Customer):** Mandatory identity verification that all banks must complete before opening an account, per RBI regulations. The digibank onboarding collects KYC information across Steps 7–14.

- **eKYC:** Electronic KYC — identity verification performed digitally using Aadhaar OTP authentication via UIDAI. The fully digital onboarding path uses eKYC. Results in a virtual debit card.

- **CKYC (Central KYC):** A centralised KYC record held by CERSAI. The digibank digital flow does not support CKYC inline; users who want to use CKYC must visit a DBS branch.

## Account Terms

- **MAB (Monthly Average Balance):** The average of your account's daily closing balances over a calendar month. Each account tier has a required MAB; falling below it triggers a fall-below fee. The exact calculation method is in DBS's product terms.

- **TRV (Total Relationship Value):** Verbatim definition from Step 17: *"TRV is across all accounts in a family and calculated by aggregating average quarterly saving account balances and end of period term deposits and investments."* Relevant to debit card fee waiver eligibility.

- **Nominee:** A person you designate to receive your account balance in the event of your death. Optional during onboarding (Step 15) but strongly recommended.

- **Debit card — virtual:** A debit card accessible only within the digibank app. Issued to customers who open a Savings Account via the OTP-based eKYC (digital) path.

- **Debit card — physical:** A plastic debit card delivered by mail. Issued to customers who open a Savings Account via biometric (branch) verification.

---

## Payments and Transfers

- **OTP (One-Time Password):** A short-lived numeric code sent by SMS used to verify your identity. In the onboarding flow, the OTP at Step 9 is sent by UIDAI's system via DBS's registered sender ID **JD-DBSBNK-S** to your Aadhaar-linked mobile number.

- **UPI (Unified Payments Interface):** An instant mobile payment system using a virtual payment address (VPA) instead of account numbers. Supported in the digibank app.

- **NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer):** Interbank transfer system operated by the RBI. Available 24x7.

- **RTGS (Real-Time Gross Settlement):** Real-time high-value interbank transfer system. Available 24x7.

- **IMPS (Immediate Payment Service):** Instant interbank transfer via NPCI. Available 24x7.

---

## Products

- **digiPortfolio:** A ready-made basket of mutual funds available to DBS Growth Plus and Growth Max account holders through the digibank app. For product details, refer to the digiPortfolio section in the app.

- **LVB (Lakshmi Vilas Bank):** A private bank that merged into DBS Bank India in November 2020. Former LVB account holders can link their account to digibank via the LINK ACCOUNT option at Step 3.
